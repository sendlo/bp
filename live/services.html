Au Pair Services

House Sitting

Part of my job while I am at your home tending to your furbabies is to care for your home. I will bring in the mail, take garbage in and out, bring in newspapers and water indoor and outdoor plants as directed. This is included in my daily fee.

Puppy Care

Puppy's need special attention for the first 4 months while they are not allowed to go outside in public places. They need to be socialized to people and other dogs. I am happy to come over and play with your puppy and do some basic obedience and potty training during the day while you are at work.

Once your puppy has had all of its vaccinations and can go outside I am able to help continue its training and socializing as needed.

Medicating

As a Veterinary Technician for 20 years I am able to medicate just about any dog or cat. I am familiar with most medications and know what to look for if they are having a negative reaction to the medication. If you have a pet that needs medication 3 times a day but you can only give it twice, I can run by your home and give it that 3rd dose of medication.

At work my fellow technicians and doctors would call me the "cat whisperer" because I was able to medicate even the grumpiest cat with the least amount of stress for the cat and me.

Grooming

I trim all toe nails, express anal glands, and can bathe your dogs at the local Pet Food Express. After nail trims I use a nail file tool called a Dremel to file down the sharp edges.

Training

I love training dogs and I am a stickler for manners! I only use positive food motivating methods and I am a big believer in the Gentle Leader and the No Jump halter to assist you in training your dogs. I will teach your puppy sit, stay, leave it, drop, and heal, and show you how to use them effectively.

I have not been certified as a dog trainer, but I am working on getting my CGC certificate with my current rescue Labrador. I also have experience training my own pets from the time I was 11 years old, including agility training for competitions.
