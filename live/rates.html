Rates

Overnight Care - $40

Two 30-minute visits per day to your home for up to 2 pets. $10 per day for each additional pet.

Add on an additonal 30-minute walk or play time for $15.

Oral medicating is included in my overnight care cost. I will also bring in the mail, take garbage in and out, bring in newspapers, and water plants as directed. If your pet needs insulin or SQ fluids I charge an additional fee.

Medicating - $10 to $15

Daily medicating if you are unable to medicate is $10.00 for once a day and $15.00 for twice a day. If your pet needs insulin or SQ fluids I charge an additional fee.

Day Walks - $20

A single day walk for 30 minutes (a non-overnighter).

Toe Nail Trim - $25

Using a nail file tool called a dremel.

Express Anal Glands - $25

Everyone's favorite job.

Taxi Services - $25

Includes pick up or delivery of your pets to a Veterinarian or Groomer in Benicia. Service to a location outside of Benicia is possible for an extra fee.

Other services

In special circumstances I will care for your pet in my home for an additional fee. But we will need to discuss this and make sure your pet gets along with my dog and my son on a case-by-case basis.
