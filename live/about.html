Professional care for your pets!

Hi, my name is Mia Sendlakowski and I want to help you care for your pet(s) in your home. After working as a Veterinary Technician for 20 years I decided it was time to retire. I left my last job at Bishop Ranch Veterinary Hospital in October of 2012. I took a year off but I found I was missing my work more and more. I thought about going back into the hospital environment but a few friends told me I should start doing some pet sitting and caring for pets in their own homes. This is how I started my company Au Pair for Pets, BeniciaPets.com.

I bring a special service to my clients. Not only do I have skills such as the ability to medicate your pets including insulin injections and SQ fluids, but I can also come give daily medications while you are at work. I can come to your home to express your pets anal glands, and I can do toe nail trims all at your convenience, you never have to leave your home.

I will care for your pets while you are on vacation, and in special circumstances I will bring your pet into my home to care for them 24/7.

I do quite a bit of physical therapy on post-surgical cases and will remove sutures from incisions.

If you are interested in my services, please send me an email. I will not post my cell phone number on the website for security reasons. If you send me an email and I do not get back to you within 24 hours, please email me again. I typically get back to clients within 12-24 hours.
